Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before you save your spot.

What ages is the Music Creation Playground for?
The workshop is designed for kids ages 2–12. Activities flex to each age group so younger and older kids both stay engaged.
When and where does it happen?
Two standalone sessions: Saturday, May 9, 2026 and Sunday, June 7, 2026 — both from 12–5 PM in Los Angeles. The exact address is sent to ticket holders about 2 weeks before the session.
What's included in the ticket price?
All instruments, all activities, hands-on songwriting and recording time, and a take-home creation. Just show up and play.
What should my child bring?
Water, a snack, and comfy clothes. We provide everything else — instruments, microphones, gear, and all creative supplies.
Do parents stay on-site?
Yes — parents stay on-site for the full 5 hours. It's a creative space where families can hang while the kids make music.
How does the sibling discount work?
$100 for one child, $180 for 2 siblings ($90 each), $360 for 3, and $440 for 4. Music is better together.
What's the refund policy?
Full refunds are available up to 4 days before the event start time. No refunds within 4 days of the event.
How many kids per session?
Each session is capped at 40 kids to keep things hands-on. Spots fill fast — reserve early to lock in your preferred date.
Can I attend both sessions?
Yes — each session is standalone and designed with fresh activities, so kids can attend one or both. Book each date separately.
How do I get in touch?
Email contact@bestsoundla.com or call 323.790.6099.
